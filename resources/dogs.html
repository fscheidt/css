Borzoi

Borzois, also called Russian Wolfhounds, were originally bred for hunting in Russia and are known for their speed and grace. The name "Borzoi" comes from the Russian word "borzyi", meaning "swift."

Border collie

A breed renowned for its intelligence, agility, and herding abilities. Border Collies are highly energetic and thrive in environments where they have tasks to do, making them excellent working dogs and companions for active families.
